Egypt

Egypt, country located in the northeastern corner of Africa

Egypt’s heartland, the Nile River valley and delta, was the home of one of the principal civilizations
of the ancient Middle East and, like Mesopotamia farther east,
was the site of one of the world’s earliest urban and literate societies

Pharaonic Egypt thrived for some 3,000 years through a series of native
dynasties that were interspersed with brief periods of foreign rule.
